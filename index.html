About Us

Ingwe Expert Safaris & Tours is led by Mr. Daniel Susiku, who has over 17 years of experience in the tourism industry. He knows Zambia and Southern Africa like the back of his hand.

Daniel was born in eastern Zambia and lives in Lusaka city. Despite spending much of his time in the bush where his heart lies because of his love for nature. He started his journey as a trainee guide until he qualified as a Professional Wildlife Safari guide , later became a Head Guide. After achieving other qualifications in management he was later promoted to Operations /activities Manager at a prestigious Lodge, he has climbed ranks to become one of the top ten wildlife Safari Guides in the country.

He's known for leading walking safaris, exciting canoe trips, and thrilling game drives. Daniel's passion for nature, including birdwatching and tracking elusive big cats, is boundless. His love for the wilderness inspired him to start his own company, Ingwe Expert Safaris & Tours, where his primary focus is moving the business to the next level.

When you sign up, you're not just benefiting from our experience; you're experiencing Africa through Daniel's eyes, and that makes your safari a truly unforgettable adventure.
